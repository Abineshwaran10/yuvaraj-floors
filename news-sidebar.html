APPLICATION PROCESS

Floor Preparation:

Design the floor as per the requirement with adequate reinforcement. Plan layout of all the conduits and joints carefully..

Pour Concrete & level:

Specify and order concrete as per the requirement. Ensure quality and smooth delivery..

Apply color:

Carefully apply the color as specified and at the appropriate time..

Finish using Power Trowels:

Take care to finish as required using power trowels and edge trowels.

Cure & Protect:

Cure for 28 days using water or a curing compound. Protect the floor during & after curing from any damage.

Grind & Polish:

Grind and polish the floor as per the finish required using appropriate machinery, tools & chemicals

Regular Maintenance:

Regular cleaning and maintenance is critical to keeping your floor looking & performing like new.
